Existing medical conditions

Your policy will not usually cover you for any existing conditions you have.

These are injuries, illnesses or diseases you or anyone on your policy had before your policy started. It does not matter if you have been diagnosed or not.

Our health insurance covers you for new issues you may have after your policy starts. This is the case with most health insurers.

This is different to a medical history. We will ask for a medical history after you have bought your policy. You'll only need to do this once.

We will tell you if anyone on your cover has any conditions that are not covered.

Your medical history will not affect the price you pay.
